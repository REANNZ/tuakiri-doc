
Registrations into Tuakiri are done [Using the Metadata Tool]({{ 'federation_management/using_metadata_tool' | relative_url}}).  However, as the Metadata Tool uses Tuakiri to authenticate, and before the IdP is registered, you will not likely be able to authenticate, please get in touch with the [Tuakiri Service Desk](mailto:reannz@tuakiri.ac.nz) to register your IdP into Tuakiri.
